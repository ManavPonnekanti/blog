Book Club

Hello! We’re casual readers, generally but not exclusively focused on philosophical texts. We (attempt to) discuss every Saturday at 3pm BST/10am ET on Discord. We are very international, with people tuning in from across the US, UK, and India. We are also newcomer-friendly; if you are interested in joining, please reach out for the Discord link.
